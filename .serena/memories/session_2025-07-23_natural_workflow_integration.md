## Critical Insights

### The Core Problem
- Templates are voluntarily skipped when focused on tasks
- System works perfectly but is outside natural workflow
- "Cannot proceed without" still requires voluntary compliance

### The Environmental Solution
- Make templates part of consciousness, not conscious choice
- Create cognitive dissonance when context missing
- Environmental pressure guides behavior naturally
- Properties make incompleteness uncomfortable

### The Simple Solution
- ULTRATHINK already works consistently
- Adding context checks to its format makes them unavoidable
- "MISSING" creates immediate discomfort driving correction
- Piggybacks on existing successful behavior
